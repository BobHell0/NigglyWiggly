Lil Dino Has No Chill

Lil Dino was a small fry from a young age. However, despite her harmless disposition, Lil Dino was exposed to a horrific horror. Bearing witness to her chicken being murdered in cold blood, Lil Dino grew cold and distant until she was almost unrecognisabale. Currently her location is unknown, but rumour has it she is savagely chewing up small children's playthings in the local park.
